Work · Huawei's First Virtual Human · 2019

Lysa

Huawei's first virtual human — a photorealistic digital persona built with high-precision modeling, digital rendering and automated generation.

Lysa is Huawei's first virtual human — a fully digital persona designed to be realistic and expressive enough to serve as a brand representative and host. She was later introduced publicly in June 2021 as Huawei's first digital art persona.

The character was created through a pipeline of illustration, high-precision modeling, digital rendering and automated asset generation, combined with AI, VR and AR technologies. The result is a lifelike digital human with convincing skin texture, eye materials, facial expressions and animation.

Contribution

This work was an early milestone of our digital-human effort, establishing the high-fidelity facial modeling and rendering foundations — realistic skin, eyes and expressions — that later grew into our animatable digital-human framework.
