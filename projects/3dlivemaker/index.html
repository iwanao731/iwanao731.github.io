Work · Huawei Mate20 Pro · 2018

3D LIVE MAKER

The first smartphone app for making your own characters by combining 3D scanning and auto-rigging.

3D LIVE MAKER is the first smartphone app for making own characters by combining 3D scanning and auto-rigging technology. The app was released for HUAWEI Mate20 Pro in Oct. 2018.

Launch Event

We demonstrated the app on the stage for audiences. I also had a demo at the hands-on booth.
